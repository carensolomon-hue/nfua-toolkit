Caption — click to copy
What's actually being proposed? Extending Atlanta's existing TADs through 2055 to fund the Neighborhood Reinvestment Initiative (NRI) at the scale it requires. That means keeping growth dollars working in the neighborhoods that generated them — especially communities that have experienced decades of disinvestment — instead of leaving outcomes to market forces alone. This is about closing real gaps: • life expectancy • income • access to opportunity And doing it with a tool that already works. Not a new tax. Not a tax increase. Not taking from schools.
