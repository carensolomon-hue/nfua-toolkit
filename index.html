End Atlanta's Tale of Two Cities
What's actually being proposed? Extending Atlanta's existing TADs through 2055 to fund the Neighborhood Reinvestment Initiative (NRI) at the scale it requires. That means keeping growth dollars working in the neighborhoods that generated them — especially communities that have experienced decades of disinvestment — instead of leaving outcomes to market forces alone. This is about closing real gaps: • life expectancy • income • access to opportunity And doing it with a tool that already works. Not a new tax. Not a tax increase. Not taking from schools. NRI and TAD Extensions Make Financial Sense for All Atlantans
TAD extension is not just about one neighborhood. It is about the whole city. TAD areas grew 193% in assessed value from 2007 to 2024, far outpacing non-TAD areas. That growth strengthens the city's tax base, funds APS, benefits Fulton County, and builds more stable communities for residents inside and outside the TADs. Extending the TADs through 2055 is a return on an investment that is already working. Learn more at www.extendthetads.com. #ExtendTheTADs #ATLNeighborhoods
